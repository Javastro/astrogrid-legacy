package: patterns.cea

Execution of a CEA-conforming service

The invocation follows the normal pattern of Axis web-services.

The execute operation is delegated by the stub to the service skeleton by SOAP and then delegated by the skeleton to the ExecutionController by a normal method-call. The parameters of this operation must be encoded as a single string which may be serialized XML.

The locator is used as a factory for the stub in order that the client does not see the stub class. The service endpoint is locked into the stub at creation.
