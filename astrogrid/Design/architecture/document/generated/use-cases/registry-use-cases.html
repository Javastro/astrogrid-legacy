UseCases
UseCase Be harvested by other registries
An AstroGrid registry is registered in other, full registries. Those registries periodically harvest the former's registrations.
UseCase Deploy registry
Registry operator creates and configures a new registry. The new registry may itself be registerd in existing, full registries and existing publishing registries may be registered in the new registry.
