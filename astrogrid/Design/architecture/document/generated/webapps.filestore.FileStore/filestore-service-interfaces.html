Class Diagram filestore-service-interfaces
package: webapps.filestore.FileStore
This service has both a compile time and run-time contract. They have WSDL port-type, binding and service elements with matchign names but are separately derived. The compile-time contract is derived from the service-definition interface. It is used in the build to generate the client stub. The run-time contract is derived from introspection of the service implementation-class.
