Set-up operations for AstroGrid registries

You must do the self-registration; otherwise your registry will not work. You need only set up the harvesting if you want to connect to the wider grid of registries.

Self registration
To enable your registry to receive registrations you must first register in it a resource of type registryType; this defines your registry. Please use the form for self registration.
Harvesting by other registries
To have your registry mirrored by other registries you must copy your registryType entry into those registries. Please use the form for harvesting by other registries.
Harvesting of other registrations
To make your registry mirror other registries you must copy their registryType entries into your registry. Please use the form for harvesting of other registries.
Adding Entries
Once your registry type is defined, you can add new entries. Please use the form for adding new entries..
