Registry Entry Form

Enter the VOResource in the text area below and press the [Submit] button to add it to the registry


This will automatically post your Registry entries on a full Registry (hydra of Astrogrid), allowing the Full Registry to know about this Registry and harvest it during its next havest cycle. This is just a simple post
This will automatically download Registry type entries from another registry and add it to this Registry. Please input the url to another registry, a default full astrogrid registry is already located in the box.

Below you will find example templates that you can copy/save and place into the text area. Please do a "View Source" in your web browser when copying.
