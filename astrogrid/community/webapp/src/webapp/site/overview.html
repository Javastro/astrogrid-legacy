Overview

The community software provides a means to register IVO users, and to provide them with credentials so that they can access restricted resources. The community is supplied as a web application that provides a number of services.

Accounts service:
a RESTful web-service that dispenses credentials and reveals the URL for a user's home space in VOSpace.
Policy manager:
a SOAP service which reports to the client the IVORN for the user's home space.
This is service is deprecated, its role being taken over by the accounts service. Clients using this service should switch to the accounts service immediately, as the policy manager will be removed in later releases.
Administration interface:
a classical web-browser interface for the community operators.

The community contains a local certificate authority, operated by the community managers through the community's administration interface.
