Index to schemata

The URIs in this list are the namespace URIs for AstroGrid schemata that are stable and published. The hyperlinks in the list point to the schema documents (.xsd, .wsdl); the target URLs of the hyperlinks are the location URIs of the schemata.

You may find this index in two places. On AstroGrid's central software-site (software.astrogrid.org), the location URIs are very stable, and are ideal for use in the schemaLocation attributes of XML documents. On AstroGrid's development site (www.astrogrid.org/maven), in the section devoted to the contracts Maven-project, the location URIs change with every new version of that project and are not suitable for use in instance documents.
